Welcome to Real Art Advocates

We are a community dedicated to preserving human creativity in art and opposing AI-generated art. Our mission is to raise awareness and engage in discussions about the impact of AI on the art world.

Our Vision

Real Art Advocates envisions a world where human-made art is celebrated, protected, and continues to be a source of inspiration for generations to come.

Join Us

Get involved in our community to support the cause. Whether you're an artist, art enthusiast, or someone who cares about the future of creativity, your contribution is valued.
